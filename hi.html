About Us

We calculate carbon footprint from

Our calculator provides a simple way to estimate your daily carbon footprint based on your trasportation,electricity used,food consumption. By inputting your daily activities you can understand your impact on the environment and recieve personalise suggestion for reduction. This simple carbon calculator doesn't enable you to track other stuff in your life like food or energy, but it does its singular job rather well - you can calculate the carbon debt of any journey by (almost) any means.
