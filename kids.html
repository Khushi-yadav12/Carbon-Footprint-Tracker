What are Greenhouse Gases?

Greenhouse gases are gases that trap heat in the Earth's atmosphere. They keep our planet warm and help make it a comfortable place to live. However, too much of these gases can lead to global warming.

Examples of Greenhouse Gases

  • Carbon Dioxide (CO₂)
  • Methane (CH₄)
  • Nitrous Oxide (N₂O)
  • Water Vapor (H₂O)
