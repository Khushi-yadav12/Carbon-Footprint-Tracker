About Us

We calculate carbon footprint from
your given data

Our Carbon Footprint Calculator leverages the power of machine learning to analyze individuals’ daily, weekly, and monthly activities, providing a comprehensive assessment of their carbon footprint from an environmentally friendly perspective. The application goes beyond conventional calculators by determining users’ tree debts to nature, offering a tangible and relatable measure of their impactOur calculator provides a simple way to estimate your daily carbon footprint based on your trasportation,electricity used,food consumption. By inputting your daily activities you can understand your impact on the environment and recieve personalise suggestion for reduction. This simple carbon calculator doesn't enable you to track other stuff in your life like food or energy, but it does its singular job rather well - you can calculate the carbon debt of any journey by (almost) any means.This app enables you to track things like the type of food you eat as well as the carbon you use getting about. It can predict your mode of transport and calculate accordingly. You can also set a weekly goal to see how you measure up against friends as well as compare your own emissions from week-to-week.
